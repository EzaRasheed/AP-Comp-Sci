After all my hard work in high school, everything paid off when I got my acceptance into my dream college, the University of Virginia, on March 22, 2017. It's going to be hard to leave my family and live independently, but atleast the drive is only 2 hours away. I plan on majoring in Computer Science through the College of Arts and Sciences. It's going to be tough, but I hope everything works out. I am both excited and nervous to leave, but I know the college experience will all be worth it once I get there. I cannot wait to decorate my dorm with my roommate and enjoy numerous of adventures with her! At this point, I am impatiently waiting for the next big step in my life at UVA, with great friends and memorable experiences! Wahoowa!
